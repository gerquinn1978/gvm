Grounded Vibe Methodology

AI can build fully engineered software. You just need the right process and the right experts.

A methodology for building well-engineered software with AI. Structure catches failures. Citation-based expert grounding defines quality. Both together produce software that ships with confidence.

Conor Brennan & Gerard Quinn
